Welcome to Laluram Halvaiwala

For decades, Laluram Halvaiwala has been delighting sweet lovers with authentic, handcrafted mithai prepared using time-tested recipes and the finest ingredients — especially pure desi ghee.

From creamy peda and melt-in-mouth barfi to rich sohan halwa and juicy gulab jamun — every piece carries the legacy of taste and tradition.

We believe in quality over quantity. No shortcuts. Just pure love in every sweet.
